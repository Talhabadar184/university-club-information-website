How to Get Involved

Ready to jump in? Follow these simple steps to become an active member of our campus community:

  1. Browse the Club Categories to find organizations that interest you.
  2. Attend a general interest meeting (check the Activities page).
  3. Reach out to the club president via their official university email.
  4. Pay any required membership dues (most academic clubs are free!).
